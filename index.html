🤖 Japanese　Business Assistant

Your smart and professional assistant for responding to customers in Japanese and Vietnamese.

📌 What is it?

ChatJapanese Vietnamese Business Assistant is a Chrome extension designed to help users generate polite, professional replies in Japanese and Vietnamese for use in business contexts, such as email, chat, or customer service scenarios.

✨ Key Features

  • Generate AI-based responses instantly
  • Switch easily between Japanese and Vietnamese
  • Designed for business environments: formal, polite tone
  • No data is stored or tracked – your privacy is respected
